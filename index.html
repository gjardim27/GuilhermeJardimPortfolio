About Me

I'm a junior studying Aerospace Engineering at the University of Southern California with a focus on aerodynamics, experimental fluid mechanics, and computational flow analysis. My work spans wind tunnel testing, particle image velocimetry (PIV), computational fluid dynamics, and data-driven analysis of complex flow phenomena.

At the Dryden Wind Tunnel Laboratory, I investigate active control strategies for transitional airfoil flows, studying how acoustic forcing influences separation behaviour at low Reynolds numbers.

I'm particularly interested in aerodynamics and flight modeling & simulation.
