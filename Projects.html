Vorticity Identification from Instantaneous PIV

Wind Tunnel Research

Developed Python scripts to extract vortex core spacing in shear layers from PIV data. Computed swirling strength (λci) and Q-criterion fields to identify coherent vortical structures. Relates spatial wavelengths to temporal frequencies via convection velocity estimates for Kelvin-Helmholtz instability characterization.
