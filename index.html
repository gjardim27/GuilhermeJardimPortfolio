About Me

I'm an Aerospace Engineering student at the University of Southern California with a focus on aerodynamics and experimental fluid mechanics. My work spans wind tunnel testing, particle image velocimetry (PIV), computational fluid dynamics, and data-driven analysis of complex flow phenomena.

Currently, I'm investigating active control of transitional airfoil flows at the Dryden Wind Tunnel, studying acoustic forcing effects on separation behavior at low Reynolds numbers.

Focus Areas Aerodynamics, CFD, PIV, Wind Tunnel Testing
Tools MATLAB, Python, DaVis, Siemens NX
