Quadcopter Drone Design

Overview

Designed and modeled a complete quadcopter drone in Siemens NX as part of AME 308 (Computer Aided Engineering). The project covered the full CAD workflow from individual component design through constrained assembly.

Design Process

Created fully constrained assemblies with exploded views and bills of materials. Performed motion and torque simulations under both friction and frictionless conditions to validate the mechanical design.

Tools
